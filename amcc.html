About AMCC

The Australian Multicultural Cricket Council is the community and competition engine behind BCL and wider multicultural cricket initiatives across Australia.

AMCC brings together players, teams and communities from diverse cultural backgrounds through competitions, events and long-term development pathways. BCL extends that mission with a more premium, league-style presentation.

The goal is to create strong community connection, better visibility for players and teams, and a more professional digital home for multicultural cricket.

Key focus areas

Growing cricket through community and presentation

  • Multicultural competitions and events
  • Community engagement and pathways
  • Player and team visibility
  • Commercial and sponsor readiness
  • Professional digital presentation for the sport
